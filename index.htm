Activities

Fishing

There arll types of great fishiing opptunities avaiable at Smith Mouatian Lake. The most popular specises fished in the all year around is the Largemouth Bass. These fish can be caught on almost any artifical bait depending on the water conditions and weather. Smith Mountain Lake has consistently produced and large number of bass and has alos been the home to ESPNs Bassmaster Elite series tournment for several years. Striped Bass is another sought after fish in this resorivor. They were introduced in 1963 and are frequently stocked to matian their population becasue the their habatit only allows for limited spawning. Channel and Flathead catfish are also abundent throught the lake, many anglers will fish after dusk for catfish.

Regulations

Virginia residents and out of state residents must have a vaild Virginia fishing license to fish in these waters. Which can be obtained by visiting the Virginia Department of Game and Inland Fisheries website: VDGIF Site Also there are bag limits on fish that can be kept check out the following link to stay up to date with the current catch limits at SML:Catch Limits

Boating

Boating is among one of the highest attractions on this lake. SML is a 20,600-acre lake that can generously accommodate a large amount of boat traffic. Weather you would like to bring your yacht or jet ski either is acceptable. A wide variety of watercraft are in use at SML. They range from sailboats, cabin cruisers, wakeboard boats, john boats, center console fishing rigs, bass boats, personal water crafts and occasionally you will spot a classic Jet Ski. There are also various rental locations on the water. They usually rent out jet ski's, boats, and watersports equipment.

Restrictions

There is no horsepower limit at SML. A Virginia boaters saftey course must be completed by all PWC operaters and motorboat operaters must complete a course depending on their age.

Water Sports

Many vacationaors and visitors pratcipate in watersports while on the lake. Wakeboaring, sking, tubing, and wake surfing are among the most popular watersports.

Places to Visit

Places to visit while in the SML area include the Dam, SML State Park, SML Beach attraction, D-day Memorial and the Booker T National Monument.
